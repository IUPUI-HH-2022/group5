Lasting Change through Diversity and Inclusion

This website is a collaboration between students from Indiana University (IUPUI) in Indianapolis, Indiana and students from Haaga-Helia University in Helsinki, Finland. Our topic is Equity & Inclusion in Education, and we have focused our efforts on three categories; Race/Ethnicity, Gender, and LGBTQ issues. We have reviewed these three categories across three countries: the United States, Finland, and New Zealand.
